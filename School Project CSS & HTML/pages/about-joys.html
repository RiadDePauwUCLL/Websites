The joys of riding a motorcycle

There's quite a lot to say about what's joyful in riding a motorcycle. Everybody likes to empty their mind & refresh their brain.

Motorcycles, what's the joy in them?

It really depends on the context on how we enjoy that specific kind of activity. Some perceive it as a hobby, some as a way of escaping reality.

It also has been proven as an anti-depressant !

Here's a few things what riding a motorcycle does to you:

  • Adrenaline
  • Emptying your thoughts
  • A breath of fresh air
  • Make up new ideas
  • A change of mind
  • Boosts confidence & self-esteem

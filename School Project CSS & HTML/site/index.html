Motorcycles & their usage

Motorcycles have existed for quite some time now and they are used for a decent amount of activities all around the world.

It is used for off-road, racing, traveling, parkour and many more. Every motorcycle is also different from eachother. Why, you ask?

A 2-stroke dirtbike for example is not the same as a 1000cc 4-stroke sportsbike. Both have their up & downs and are comparable. A 2-stroke requires more maintenance compared to a 4-stroke, as the engine functions more faster & wears off easily.

What is the best motorcycle to ride with?

It's a subjective manner that we are talking about here.

A sports bike isn't the same thing compared to an offroad one, as they are both used for different reasons. It all depends on where, how and when you ride & what exactly is the purpose of riding it.
